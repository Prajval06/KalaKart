Design a clean, minimal, and highly intuitive web dashboard for local artisans selling handmade products. The users are non-technical, so the interface must be extremely simple, clear, and action-oriented.

The goal is to help artisans quickly manage products, orders, and earnings while also guiding them to sell better through smart suggestions.

OVERALL LAYOUT:

* Use a simple layout with a fixed left sidebar and a top navigation bar
* Below the top bar, include a “Smart Assistant Bar” that displays dynamic, helpful messages
* Main content area changes based on selected section
* Design should be mobile-friendly and responsive

SIDEBAR (ONLY 4 ITEMS):

* Home
* Products
* Orders
* Earnings

TOP NAVBAR:

* Search bar (for products and orders)
* Notification bell icon
* User profile icon
* Primary CTA button: “+ Add Product”

SMART ASSISTANT BAR (CORE DIFFERENTIATOR):

* Positioned below the top navbar
* Displays 1–2 short, actionable messages at a time
* Examples:
  “You have 2 new orders to ship today”
  “Your product is low on stock”
  “Add more images to improve sales”
  “Your sales increased this week”
* Messages should be simple, human-readable, and helpful

HOME PAGE (MAIN DASHBOARD):

* Show 4 KPI cards:
  Total Earnings, Orders, Products, Alerts
* Add two large primary action buttons:
  “Add Product” and “View Orders”
* Highlight “New Orders” prominently at the top with quick action buttons (Accept)
* Show recent activity:
  low stock alerts, recent sales updates
* Include small visual tags like “Low Stock” or “Selling Fast”

PRODUCTS PAGE:

* Header with “+ Add Product” button and search
* Display products in a simple table:
  Image, Name, Price, Stock, Status, Actions
* Actions: Edit, Delete
* Include stock indicators (Low Stock / Out of Stock)

ADD/EDIT PRODUCT EXPERIENCE:

* Fields: name, price, stock, category, description, image upload
* Show real-time smart suggestions:
  “Image looks blurry”
  “Add at least 2 images”
  “Your price is higher than similar products”

ORDERS PAGE:

* Tabs: New, Processing, Completed
* Highlight new orders at the top
* Orders table:
  Order ID, Product, Amount, Status, Actions
* Actions: Accept, Mark as Shipped, Mark as Completed
* Show helpful warnings like:
  “Ship within 24 hours”

EARNINGS PAGE:

* Show summary:
  Total Earned, Available Balance
* Simple transaction list:
  Date, Order ID, Amount
* Show small insights like:
  “You earned 20% more this week”
  “Top earning product”

NOTIFICATIONS:

* Accessible via bell icon
* Include alerts for new orders, low stock, and payments
* Also surface important alerts on the Home page

MICRO FEATURE (EMOTIONAL TOUCH):

* Include a “Store Story” section:
  short description of the artisan’s work
* Allow editing this text easily

DESIGN STYLE:

* Clean, minimal, and modern
* Warm and slightly handcrafted feel (not corporate)
* Use soft colors, rounded cards, and subtle shadows
* Large buttons and readable typography
* Use icons alongside text labels

UX PRINCIPLES:

* The user should understand what to do within 5 seconds
* Minimize clicks (max 2–3 steps for key actions)
* Avoid technical terms and complex data
* Prioritize clarity over feature richness

DO NOT INCLUDE:

* Complex analytics dashboards
* Heavy charts or graphs
* Advanced filters or multi-level navigation
* Technical jargon like conversion rate

GOAL:
Create a simple but powerful dashboard that not only helps artisans manage their shop but also guides them with smart, human-like suggestions to improve their sales.
