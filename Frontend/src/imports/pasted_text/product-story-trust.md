Design and implement a product page experience that:

Builds emotional connection before purchase

Establishes deep trust and authenticity

Differentiates from generic e-commerce platforms like Amazon/Etsy

🔶 SECTION 1: STORY LAYER (POINT 2)
🧩 Goal:

Transform every product from a “listing” into a story-driven experience

🧱 COMPONENT: “Product Story Section” (Above Add to Cart)
📌 Structure:

Title (Emotional Hook)

Example:

“Crafted over 2 days using traditional मिट्टी techniques”

“A heritage weave passed through 3 generations”

Story Block (Short Narrative)

80–120 words max

Must include:

Origin (village/state)

Cultural significance

Effort/time involved

Tone: Human, emotional, not corporate

🎥 COMPONENT: “Behind the Craft” (Visual Section)
📌 UI Elements:

Horizontal scroll OR video block

3–5 steps visual storytelling

📌 Content:

Step 1: Raw material

Step 2: Crafting process

Step 3: Detailing

Step 4: Final product

📌 Labels:

“Step 1: मिट्टी sourcing from local riverbeds”

“Step 2: Hand-shaping by artisan”

⏱ COMPONENT: “Effort & Time Indicator”
📌 Display:

Icons + short text

📌 Example:

⏳ 2 Hours to Make

👨‍🎨 Handmade by 1 Artisan

🧵 100% Handcrafted

🌍 COMPONENT: “Cultural Context Tag”
📌 Placement:

Below story block

📌 Example:

“Origin: Rajasthan”

“Craft Type: Terracotta Art”

“Occasion: Diwali / Festive Decor”

🚫 STRICT RULES:

No generic product descriptions

No copy-paste supplier content

Every product must have a unique story

🔷 SECTION 2: TRUST SYSTEM (POINT 6)
🧩 Goal:

Eliminate buyer doubt and prove authenticity + fairness

✅ COMPONENT: “Verified Artisan Badge”
📌 UI:

Badge near product title

📌 Label:

“✔ Verified Artisan Product”

📌 On Click:

Show modal with:

Artisan verification process

Location proof

Craft authenticity validation

👤 COMPONENT: “Artisan Identity Card”
📌 Placement:

Directly below product title or near images

📌 Content:

Artisan Name

Village/City

Years of experience

Small circular photo

📌 Example:

“Made by Ramesh Kumar, Jaipur”

“15 years of experience”

🎥 COMPONENT: “Proof of Craft” (Trust Video/Image)
📌 Content:

Short clip OR real photos of artisan working

📌 Purpose:

Remove doubt: “Is this really handmade?”

💰 COMPONENT: “Transparent Pricing Breakdown”
📌 UI:

Simple expandable section

📌 Example:

Artisan earns: ₹800

Platform fee: ₹200

Logistics: ₹200

📌 Label:

“See where your money goes”

📍 COMPONENT: “Origin Verification”
📌 Display:

Map pin OR text

📌 Example:

“Sourced directly from: Kutch, Gujarat”

⭐ COMPONENT: “Authenticity Guarantee”
📌 Badge/Text:

“100% Handmade or Money Back Guarantee”

🚫 STRICT RULES:

No fake badges

No stock images for artisan

No vague claims like “premium quality”

Everything must feel real, provable, human

🎨 UI/UX GUIDELINES (IMPORTANT)

Use warm, earthy colors (not Amazon-style white/blue overload)

Typography should feel cultural + modern

Avoid clutter—focus on storytelling flow

Prioritize:

Story

Artisan

Trust

Then purchase

⚙️ FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

CMS support for:

Adding story text per product

Uploading artisan data

Uploading process images/videos

Dynamic fields:

Time to make

Craft type

Location

🧠 FINAL POSITIONING (DO NOT IGNORE)

This page should NOT feel like:
❌ “Buy product”

It should feel like:
✅ “Understand → Connect → Trust → Then Buy”
